About

I'm a Principal Product Manager with 15+ years of experience driving end-to-end digital transformation across eCommerce, FinTech, ElderTech, and Utilities. I've led product strategy across iconic brands including The North Face, Vans, Timberland, and Williams-Sonoma.

I'm now focused on the intersection of AI and product management — building, evaluating, and shipping AI-powered systems that create measurable business outcomes. This portfolio showcases 12 AI agents I designed and deployed across Retail, Retail SaaS, and FinTech domains.

Each agent demonstrates a core concept in applied AI — from RAG and guardrails to multi-step orchestration and multimodal analysis — built entirely through vibe coding using Claude.ai.
