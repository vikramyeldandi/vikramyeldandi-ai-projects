## 1. ACCOUNTS

### 1.1 Opening an Account
- Finsera accounts are available to UK residents aged 18 and over.
- Identity verification is required: valid passport or driving licence plus proof of address.
- Verification typically takes 24–48 hours. Some accounts are approved instantly.
- There is no minimum opening balance.

### 1.2 Account Types
- **Finsera Essential** — Free. Includes UK current account, sort code and account number, Visa debit card, and in-app payments.
- **Finsera Plus** — £9.99/month. Includes everything in Essential, plus multi-currency wallet (30+ currencies), no foreign transaction fees, and priority support.
- **Finsera Premium** — £19.99/month. Includes everything in Plus, plus metal debit card, travel insurance, airport lounge access (via LoungeKey), and a dedicated relationship manager.

### 1.3 Account Limits
- ATM withdrawals: £300/day (Essential), £500/day (Plus/Premium)
- Card payments: £5,000/day (Essential), £10,000/day (Plus/Premium)
- Incoming transfers: No limit
- Outgoing transfers: £20,000/day (verified accounts); £1,000/day (unverified)

### 1.4 Closing an Account
- Accounts can be closed in-app under Settings → Account → Close Account.
- Any remaining balance must be transferred out before closing.
- Closure takes up to 5 business days.
- Finsera will send a closure confirmation to the registered email address.

### 1.5 Updating Personal Details
- Name changes require legal documentation (e.g. deed poll or marriage certificate).
- Address and phone number can be updated in-app under Settings → Profile.
- Email address changes require verification from both old and new email addresses.

## 2. PAYMENTS & TRANSFERS

### 2.1 Domestic Payments (UK)
- Faster Payments: sent within 2 hours, usually instant. Free for all accounts.
- CHAPS: same-day guaranteed settlement. Fee: £15 per transfer.
- Standing orders: set up in-app under Payments → Standing Orders. Free.
- Direct debits: managed in-app under Payments → Direct Debits.

### 2.2 International Payments
- Available to Plus and Premium accounts only.
- SEPA (Europe): 1–2 business days. Fee: £1.50 per transfer.
- SWIFT (Worldwide): 2–5 business days. Fee: £5.00 per transfer.
- Exchange rates: mid-market rate with no markup for Plus/Premium. Essential accounts pay a 1.5% foreign exchange fee.
- Transfer limits: £50,000 per international transfer (additional verification may be required above £10,000).

### 2.3 Requesting Money
- Use the Finsera Pay link feature: Payments → Request Money → Share Link.
- Recipients do not need a Finsera account to pay via link.
- Payment links expire after 72 hours.

### 2.4 Scheduled Payments
- Payments can be scheduled up to 60 days in advance.
- Scheduled payments can be edited or cancelled in-app until 11pm on the day before the payment date.

### 2.5 Failed Payments
- Payments fail if there are insufficient funds, the recipient details are incorrect, or the transfer is blocked by a security check.
- Failed payment fees: £0 for the first failure per month; £5 for each subsequent failure.
- Customers receive an in-app notification and email when a payment fails.

## 3. CARDS

### 3.1 Card Types
- **Virtual card:** available immediately upon account opening. Use for online purchases.
- **Physical card:** dispatched within 3–5 business days. Standard delivery is free. Express delivery (next business day): £4.99.
- **Metal card:** available to Premium account holders only.

### 3.2 Activating a Card
- Physical cards are activated in-app: Cards → Activate Card → Enter last 4 digits.
- Virtual cards are active immediately.

### 3.3 Freezing and Unfreezing a Card
- Cards can be frozen instantly in-app: Cards → Freeze Card.
- Frozen cards cannot be used for any transactions, including contactless.
- Cards can be unfrozen at any time with no fee.

### 3.4 Lost or Stolen Cards
- Report immediately via the app (Cards → Report Lost/Stolen) or by calling 0800 999 1234 (24/7).
- The card will be cancelled and a replacement dispatched within 3–5 business days.
- Replacement card fee: £0 for the first replacement per year; £5 for each subsequent replacement.

### 3.5 Card Disputes
- Disputes must be raised within 120 days of the transaction date.
- Submit via in-app: Cards → Transactions → [Select transaction] → Dispute.
- Finsera investigates within 5–10 business days and may issue a provisional credit while the investigation is ongoing.
- If the dispute is found in the customer's favour, the full amount is refunded. If not, the provisional credit is reversed.

### 3.6 Contactless Payments
- Contactless limit: £100 per transaction.
- After 5 consecutive contactless transactions, PIN verification is required.

### 3.7 PIN
- PINs can be viewed in-app: Cards → View PIN (requires biometric authentication).
- PINs can be changed at any ATM that supports PIN change, or in-app.

## 4. SECURITY & FRAUD

### 4.1 Fraud Reporting
- If a customer suspects fraud or unauthorised transactions, they should:
  1. Freeze their card immediately in-app.
  2. Call the 24/7 fraud line: 0800 999 1234.
  3. Report via in-app: Security → Report Fraud.
- Finsera will investigate and respond within 24 hours.
- Under FCA regulations, customers are protected from unauthorised transactions provided they have not acted fraudulently or with gross negligence.

### 4.2 Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)
- 2FA is mandatory for all Finsera accounts.
- Authentication methods: SMS OTP, authenticator app (Google Authenticator, Authy), or biometric (Face ID / fingerprint).
- 2FA can be managed in-app under Settings → Security → Two-Factor Authentication.

### 4.3 Phishing and Scam Awareness
- Finsera will NEVER ask for full passwords, PINs, or one-time passcodes via phone, email, or chat.
- If a customer receives a suspicious call, email, or message claiming to be from Finsera, they should not share any information and should contact support immediately.
- Authorised Push Payment (APP) scams: if a customer has been tricked into authorising a payment, they should report it immediately. Finsera follows the UK Payment Systems Regulator (PSR) reimbursement guidelines.

### 4.4 Account Security Best Practices
- Use a strong, unique password for the Finsera app.
- Enable biometric authentication.
- Never share login credentials with anyone, including family members.
- Review account activity regularly via the app.

## 6. APP & TECHNICAL SUPPORT

### 6.1 Supported Devices
- iOS 14 and above (iPhone 6s and newer)
- Android 8.0 and above

### 6.2 App Issues
- If the app is crashing: force close, clear cache, and reopen. If the issue persists, uninstall and reinstall.
- If unable to log in: use Forgot Password on the login screen. If 2FA is lost, contact support with identity verification.
- Notifications: enabled in-app under Settings → Notifications.

### 6.3 Browser / Web Access
- Finsera does not currently offer a web dashboard. All account management is via the mobile app.
- A web portal is planned for Q3 2026.

## 8. ESCALATION TRIGGERS (INTERNAL — FOR AGENT USE)

The following situations must be escalated immediately to a human agent. Do not attempt to resolve these in chat:

1. **Suspected fraud or unauthorised transactions** — Direct to freeze card + 0800 999 1234 immediately.
2. **Account takeover / compromised credentials** — Direct to 0800 999 1234 immediately.
3. **Authorised Push Payment (APP) scam** — Direct to fraud line immediately.
4. **Threats of self-harm or distress** — Respond with empathy, provide Samaritans: 116 123.
5. **Legal threats or formal complaints** — Direct to complaints@finsera.com.
6. **Regulatory or compliance investigations** — Do not engage. Direct to compliance@finsera.com.
